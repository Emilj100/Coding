My Coding Journey

Not long ago, I embarked on my journey into the world of programming through the CS50 courses. I started with Python, and since then, my curiosity has only grown. Whether it’s solving challenging problems or building creative projects, coding has quickly become one of my greatest passions. My goal is to master programming languages, explore new technologies, and eventually build projects that make a real impact – maybe even launch my own business one day.

A Balance of Mind and Body

When I’m not writing lines of code, you’ll often find me out on my inline skates, pushing my limits and enjoying the freedom of gliding through the streets. Fitness is a big part of my life – I combine strength training and cardio to stay sharp, both mentally and physically. It’s about finding balance and constantly improving myself, one step (or skate!) at a time.
